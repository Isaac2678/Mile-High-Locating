Our Services

Mile High Locating specializes in private utility locating — the lines that 811 will never mark. We use professional electromagnetic locating equipment to accurately identify and mark underground utilities before you dig.

General Private Line Locating

Not sure what's buried on a property? We conduct a thorough sweep to identify and mark all detectable private utilities before excavation begins. Ideal for contractors starting work on unfamiliar sites or homeowners planning any ground disturbance.

What's Included

  • Full-site electromagnetic sweep
  • Surface marking with standard utility color codes
  • Verbal walkthrough of findings
  • Suitable for pre-construction, landscaping, fencing, and general excavation

Private Gas Line Locating

Private gas lines — including service laterals from the meter to the home and propane lines — are not covered by 811. Striking a private gas line is one of the most dangerous and costly utility strikes possible. We locate and mark private gas infrastructure accurately before any nearby digging.

What's Included

  • Locates gas lines from meter to point of use
  • Identifies propane supply lines
  • Critical for excavation near structures
  • Helps satisfy due diligence requirements under CRS 9-1.5

Secondary Electric Locating

Secondary electric lines run from the utility transformer or meter base to outbuildings, garages, shops, panels, and outdoor equipment. These are privately owned and entirely invisible to 811. We trace and mark secondary electric lines so your crew knows exactly where they run.

What's Included

  • Locates lines from meter/transformer to secondary structures
  • Identifies service feeds to outbuildings, garages, and outdoor panels
  • Common need on rural properties, farms, and commercial sites with multiple structures
