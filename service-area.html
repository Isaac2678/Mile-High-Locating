Serving All of Colorado

Mile High Locating provides private utility locating services statewide. Whether you're on the Front Range, in the mountains, on the Western Slope, or in rural Eastern Colorado — we'll come to your site.

Front Range — Primary Coverage

Our primary coverage area is the Front Range corridor — from Fort Collins to Pueblo — where demand for private locates is highest. For projects outside this area, we're still available; travel fees may apply depending on distance. Call us to discuss your location.
