Why Private Locates Matter

811 Has Limits

Calling 811 only marks public utility lines. Private lines — like irrigation, secondary electric, and private gas — are invisible to 811 and pose the greatest strike risk on private property.

Strikes Are Costly

A single utility strike can mean project shutdowns, repair bills, liability exposure, and serious injury. A private locate takes hours — delays and damages take weeks.

Colorado Law & You

Colorado's Underground Damage Prevention Law (CRS 9-1.5) requires excavators to take reasonable precautions. Private locates are your documentation that you did.
